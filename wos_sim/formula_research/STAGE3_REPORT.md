## 1. Method (non-fitting, guardrail-compliant)

Each family proposes `rate = C ¬∑ ctr_att ¬∑ core(offense; defense)`, where `core`
is a fixed functional form and `C` is a single global scale. Structural
constants inside `core` (exponents, ratio constants `k`) are **enumerated over
simple rationals**, never regressed. For a fixed `(core, constants)` the family
is **ACCEPTED iff one C lands the predicted rate inside every clean row's exact
winner-rate interval simultaneously** ‚Äî i.e. the intersection
`‚à©_i [lo_i / (ctr_i¬∑core_i), hi_i / (ctr_i¬∑core_i)]` is non-empty. Because the
interval bounds *are* the reported turn band, membership == exact blind
prediction within `turns_lo..turns_hi`. This is a feasibility test, not an
optimisation: no aggregate error is ever minimised (guardrail 2). Loser one-sided
bounds add `C¬∑ctr_loser¬∑core_loser_i < ub_i` (same C, reverse direction).
Observed outcomes are never fed into predictions (guardrail 3).

The rejection is **not** a turn-precision artifact. Widening every band by

### 3.1 Why no monomial can work (analytic, confirms the search)

Two clean facts collide:

- **Local (Exp4 near T1):** rate is ~linear in attacker Attack and Lethality and
  ~inverse in target Defense and Health ‚Äî exponents ‚âà (a,b,p,q) = (1,1,1,1)
  (`stage3` diagnostic: +10 % A ‚Üí +11.16 % rate; +10 % L ‚Üí +9.76 % rate;
  +10 % L & +10 % D cancel; +10 % A & +10 % H cancel).
- **Global (tier mirror):** rate is ~flat (‚âà0.00378) while Attack and Lethality
  both grow ‚àù tier. With exponents (1,1,1,1) the numerator `A¬∑L` grows ‚àù tier¬≤
  but the true rate is flat, so the fitted scale C must collapse
  **0.081 ‚Üí 0.009** across T1‚Ä¶T6. Directly measured; monomial dead.

Exact tier-invariance is impossible for *any* monomial: constancy of
`(1.02t)^a¬∑t^b / ((3+t)^p¬∑(5+t)^q)` across t requires
`(a+b)/t = p/(3+t) + q/(5+t)` for all t, which has no solution but the trivial
one. The observed flatness is therefore approximate, and the +3/+5 tier offsets
in D,H mean any flattening term must carry matching offsets ‚Äî only the
*defender's* A/L scale ‚àù tier, but putting those in the denominator kills the
locally-required D,H dependence. No enumerated ratio/relative form resolves this.

### 3.2 Precision footnote (honest)

The 4 pure tier-mirror rate bands *touch* at exactly **0.0037879** but their
common intersection is **empty** (T1 & T6 pinned at 264 turns ‚Üí rate ‚â• 0.0037879;
T2 & T3 recorded 265‚Äì267 ‚Üí rate < 0.0037879, open). So even a *perfectly*
tier-invariant core misses by one ULP of turn-quantization. This particular miss
is a precision artifact ‚Äî but ¬ß3 shows the forms fail by ‚â´¬±3 turns elsewhere, so
it is not the reason for rejection.

### 6.1 One hard identical-input contradiction ‚Äî L32 vs L69

contradictory:

uncaptured variable exists (deployment order? first-move? a mis-recorded run?).
Both members are excluded from the `FULL_CLEAN` gate; neither is used to reject a
family.

## 7. Discriminating experiments (to break the deadlock)

The corpus cannot separate the competing per-unit forms because **within a class,
Attack, Lethality and tier all move together (collinear)**, and the only stat
isolation (Exp4) is all at T1 (local). Proposed controlled runs, in priority
order:

1. **De-collinearise Attack vs Lethality at multiple tiers.** Deploy a T5 unit
   with Lethality manually pinned to the T1 value (and vice-versa), at 2‚Äì3 tiers.
   This is the single most valuable experiment: it pins the A-exponent and
   L-exponent *globally*, resolving the ¬ß3.1 local-vs-global contradiction.
2. **Clean fixed-mid-tier-defender attack sweep with exact turn capture.** Re-run
   the vs-T2 ladder (T3‚Ä¶T6 vs T2) recording *exact* turn counts (not ¬±1 ranges)
   to resolve the sub-linear-attack curvature that ¬ß4 shows a 2-parameter
   saturating form misses by only ~0.5 %.
3. **Replicate the L32/L69 setup (T5Inf vs T1Inf) √ó5** to determine the true
   turn count and identify the hidden variable behind the 79‚Äì81 vs 67‚Äì69 split.
4. **Re-run T4/T5/T6 vs T1 with exact turn capture** to confirm or refute the
   ¬ß6.2 non-monotonicity (is T6vT1 = 96 real, or a mis-record?).
5. **Isolate Health from Defense across tiers:** bump *only* the target's Health
   at several tiers to pin the H-exponent independently of D.
